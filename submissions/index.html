Submissions

Submissions are open for Issue I of The Monad. We are accepting poetry, short fiction, critical essays, and book reviews. Translations are also welcome.

Poetry

  • Send no more than 3-5 poems in a single document.
  • Send PDFs for poems that are married to their formatting, but poems bearing the shapes of things go against our unstated values
  • Form and harmony are always welcome
  • Prose poems are welcome too

Fiction

  • We accept short stories, flash fiction or any piece of fictional prose up to 3500 words
  • Currently we are not publishing excerpts, the submissions have to be standalone works

Essays

  • We accept critical essays, book reviews, photo essays or other non-fiction provided there is a strong link to Art
